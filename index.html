Debonairs Pizza Specials – What’s New in 2025?

Your complete guide to Debonairs’ latest deals, combos, and discounts

If there’s one thing South Africans love, it’s grabbing a hot, cheesy pizza from Debonairs. For years, this local favorite has been dishing out meals that are big on flavor and easy on the wallet. But what really keeps customers coming back are the specials. In 2025, Debonairs has rolled out a fresh set of deals that combine value, variety, and that signature taste the brand is known for.

Why Debonairs Specials Matter

With rising food costs, families are watching budgets closely. Specials aren’t just about saving money—they’re about enjoying meals without compromise. Debonairs has created a wide range of offers that suit every occasion, from weekday lunches to weekend feasts.

The Return of the Real Deal

The Real Deal is back in 2025, now with slightly bigger portions and more topping choices like BBQ Chicken and Spicy Pepperoni. Students especially love this affordable range.

Two for One Tuesdays

Every Tuesday, you get two pizzas for the price of one. In 2025, more classic and premium pizzas are included, making this deal even better for sharing.

Family Feast Combos

Perfect for families, these bundles include pizza, sides, drinks, and dessert. Popular picks are chicken wings, cheese grillers, and “Death by Chocolate” brownies.

Student and Lunch Deals

Smaller pizzas or pizza wraps paired with a drink make quick, budget-friendly meals. Pizza wraps have become especially popular for being portable and tasty.

Online-Only Specials

Exclusive app and website deals include discounts on bulk orders, upgrades, and limited-time flavors you won’t find in-store.

Seasonal & Limited-Time Offers

Debonairs now offers rotating specials tied to events like Valentine’s Day and big football matches, keeping the menu fresh and exciting.

Customer Favorites in 2025

  • Real Deal Margherita – simple yet filling
  • Two for One Meat Pizzas – perfect for sharing
  • Family Feast with Wings – a complete dinner
  • Lunch Pizza Wraps – portable and affordable
  • Online Combo Discounts – app-exclusive deals

Final Thoughts

Debonairs Pizza specials in 2025 are designed for every kind of customer—from students counting rands to families looking for easy weeknight meals. With classics like the Real Deal and Two for One Tuesdays, plus new twists like online-only deals and seasonal offers, Debonairs continues to deliver meals that feel like more than fast food—they feel like a treat.
